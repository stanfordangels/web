ENTREPRENEURS

We invite you to apply to pitch to our angel investor members for your startup's next fundraise. We run a pitch session every other month and select start-ups to pitch at the events. We accept applications on a rolling basis and will notify applicants if they are selected to pitch.

We look for seed stage startups that have revenue and that have customers either signed up or with a letter of intent (LOI). Startups should submit a deck that explains the product or service they have created, outlines the go-to-market and sales strategy, analyzes the competiton and the startup's advantages, shows how intellectual property will be protected, and includes financial projections for the next 2-3 years. The deck should include details about the raise and valuation. We prioritize startups with a Stanford alum or student who is a co-founder or a part of the team.

We also invite founders to hear the latest from thought leaders in entrepreneurship, angel investing, venture capital, law or banking as well as experts in sectors such as AI or biotech. You can follow us on LinkedIn to receive notices for upcoming remote events and programming. You may also bookmark our events page.
